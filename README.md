##  Green_Miles_App

###  Overview

The **Carbon Tracking App** is an Android application designed to help users monitor their daily travel emissions and promote sustainable transportation choices. The app uses **GPS tracking**, **carbon emission calculations**, and **gamification features** (credits, leaderboard) to encourage eco-friendly behavior.


###  Objectives

### Primary Objective

Develop a functional Android app within **12 weeks** that:
* Tracks trips using GPS
* Calculates carbon emissions
* Encourages sustainable travel through rewards

### Business Goals

* Increase awareness of environmental impact
* Promote eco-friendly habits
* Build a reward-based ecosystem
* Support CO₂ reduction initiatives
